Tuusula has some nice places and attractions, like lake tuusula. Under this you can see a photo of lake tuusula from above.





Tuusula has 19 different areas. Those are Jokela, Perttu, Lepola, Kellokoski, Jäniksenlinna, Kaunisnummi, Roinila, Ruskela, Rantatie, Tuomala, Vaunukangas, Hyrylä, Mattila, Lahela, Riihikallio, Sula, Rykmentinpuisto, Ruotsinkylä and Maantiekylä





Quite famous attraction in Tuusula is Kellokoski's mental hospital museum at kellokoski. It's close to Kellokoski's Alepa, K-supermarket and "kellokoski's ruukki" pig iron factory and a church. The mental hospitals old staff used to do for example lobotomy surgery. There was also a woman that wasn't from kellokoski's surroundings. She thought she was a princess and managed to get whole kellokoski to play it with her. There is books and movies about the princess of kellokoski.


Another is Ainola. It's Sean SIbeliukse' old home. It was builded in 1904. Nowdays it's a museum. It's not the only old building of a famous person that has other meanings now. There is also Halosenniemi. It's the old wilderness studio of artist Pekka Halonen. It was builded in 1902.


Famous event in Tuusula is around lake tuusula cycling. The idea is that many people cycle around the lake tuusula. It's about 24 kilometers long. The route is easy with a map or a phone application.


Now to the geographical things about Tuusula. It has border seven different neighbors. Those are Hyvinkää, Mäntsälä, Järvenpää, Sipoo, Kerava, Vantaa and Nurmijärvi. Tuusulas area is 225,4 squarekilometers and Lake tuusulas area is 6 squarekilometers.
